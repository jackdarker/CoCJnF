Thoughts on Combat-AI

Whats the problem?

The enemys behaviour should be dependent on:

  • equipment and morphstate of the player
  • my equipment (available potions,spells, skills )
  • what actions the player uses (f.e. relying on light or heavy attacks)

Furthermore when the partysize is >1:

  • how I could possibly support other comrades (f.e. healing/ boosting ally)
  • who of the players-party can I cause the most damage

MinMaxPlanning

Get all possible actions and counteractions to build a tree and estimate which branch of the tree is the optimum

Problematic because there might be many possible actions and because of randomness, a static tree is not possible

Rule Engine (Rete)

Define Rules and use backward chaining to get to the final goal, killing the player.

...Rules Engine are not meant for decisiion making ???

Goal Oriented Action Planing

Define SubGoals and Actions to fullfill them and reiterate other them.

...???

How do we select the actions? We need to know the costs to effect ratio.

This is especially difficult for actions that

  • take multiple turns to finish; is it better to use 2 simple attacks or 1 power attack caharged for 2 turns?
  • actions that dont deal damage directly, instead are supporting in nature or deal non-damaging effects like stun.

Estimate effect-cost ratio for

  • damage-actions: possible damage/ Total HP in relation to used AP and additional resources
  • restoration-actions: possible restoration/ Total HP in relation to used AP and additional resources
  • support-actions, f.e. shielding someone; boosting damage; :
  • nondamage-actions, f.e. blinding; disarm:
  • combined-actions , f.e leeching HP:

additionally we can depend on the job-preference of the NPC. F.e. a Fighter with Berserk-Job might prefer Power-Attacks and dont think about healing himself or others. While a Fighter with Paladin-Job might be more on the defensive side and also heals allys.
