Navigation in Overworld

The player might navigate from tile to tile.

Before leaving a tile:

  • Under certain conditions a check might be done before leaving the area, giving the user a choice to stay or continue. F.e. reminding him that he has still optional quest targets when leaving a quest area.

When entering a tile:

  • The player might need to pass several checks if he can enter the tile, f.e. if he has a certain key or gadget. If not he gets a notification and remains on the old tile.
  • Entering a tile might trigger a conditional battle or has a high chance to trigger a random battle. If the player gets defeated, he is remains on the old tile.

Fast travel

No fasttravel: Fast travel could break quests by bypassing tiles with quest triggers.

Navigation in Combat

  • The player can not navigate in BattleMode except trying to flee
