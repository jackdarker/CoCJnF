This view represents a large dungeon or overworld where the player move from one tile to the next.

Elements on this view are:

  • a clock showing the actual daytime
  • the map-grid itslef
  • a button menue for selecting actions
  • additional text-box showing info about the selected tile

Map-Grid

  • quad-grid in top down view
  • Navigation only N/S/W/E
  • the actual player location is indicated with a player avatar
  • arrows are used to move the selection marker
  • when Fire-Key is pressed the button menu opens up and lists the possible actions for this tile

Additional signs are used for special tiles:

Lock

  • indicates that the player cannot move on this tile until requirements are satisfied, f.e."Blue key in inventory"
  • the requirement is shown in the info box
  • the select marker can move on this tile and beyond

Exclamation mark

  • indicates a tile with important content f.e. quest-target, boss
  • show some hint in the info box

Door

  • indicates an entry/exit of dungeon

Camp

  • indicates a save location, f.e. player campsite, village
  • similiar to door

hidden

  • tiles might be hidden until the player moves onto them or they are revealed otherwise, f.e. by buying a map
  • no other indicators are shown for those tiles

Action-Menu

  • a list of buttons for selecting actions depending on the selcted tile
  • has a back button to close the list

Possible actions:

Move here / Explore /Visit

  • if the select marker is right next to a tile, the player try to move on this tile executing a explore-Move
  • if the target tile is a door, the player will enter/exit the dungeon
  • if the select marker is on the player tile and not camp, the player will do exploration/scavaging
  • if the select marker is on the player tile and camp, the camp view will be opened

Inventory

  • shows the inventory where items can be used

Rest

  • sleep until morning
  • only at not camp tiles

Flee

  • emergency escape to camp of player choice

Info-Box

  • shows information of the tile: Name
  • shows other info (see above)
  • shows possible cchances of encounters, f.e. "30% occurence of 2 or less weak enemys; 10% chance of finding ingridients"

Clock

  • 24h format or day-night cycle?
  • updated with each turn
