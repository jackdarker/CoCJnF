A scene is created from components:

  • ScenePersistance (done automatical by calling loadAdditive from ScenePersistance)
  • UI prefab f.e. UI_Overworld
  • the level data: models, background images, tiles,...

The starting scene of the game is ScenePersistance:

  • has a FadeToBlack-Image that is used when switching scenes
  • has a SceneController-Script that is used to swicth/load scenes
  • has a Dataservice-Script that is used to load/save SaveGame

Game-Scenes will be loaded additive and can therefore use the ScenePersistance components (SaveGame) to setup themself.
