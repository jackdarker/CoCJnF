Capturing Creatures and Training

This feature is added later.

Capturing Creatures

  • The player can capture creatures in BattleMode
  • those creatures are handled like PersistantNPC
  • more creature slots/housing might get unlocked while quests progress
  • creatures can be sold; their price depends on their training level and rareness

The process of capturing is like this:

  • the player has to find the creature and start a battle with capture mode on (selected before first round)
  • capture mode reduces the damage to the creature and the creature might flee from combat
  • some creatures might be uncapturable or untrainable, f.e. most NPCs
  • he has to find a weak spot by unlocking the full "Sense"-Level or gathering information otherwise f.e. from a trainer
  • he has to make use of the weak spot to drain the fatigue of the creature without damagin it to much
  • the creature should also not be enraged
  • when the creature is drained enough, the player might use some capture gear to bind it
  • success chance depends on?? minigame??
  • the gear might get damaged or destroyed on fail
  • on success the creature is enraged but cannot fight properly, some fatigue is restored
  • he needs to calm the creature before it can break; this can be done with potions or other things,
  • if this fails the creature will break free, destroying the gear, and flee
  • after a successful calm, the creature is stored in inventory.This will highly increase the burden
  • there is a chance on every turn that the creature escapes unless it is put in a housing

Housing Creatures

  • special housings might be needed in the players camp to store the creature, their space is limited
  • the capture gear is refund
  • the creature will decay if the player doesnt take care of it once in a while; f.e. reducing skill, starving

Training Creatures

  • to be able to use the creature in combat they need a basic training/ to be tamed
  • there skill might further enhance with each combat and interation with the player
  • training requires appropiate skilllevels for the trainer
  • training is done with a minigame??

Using Creatures

  • the player can take a creature with him if he has a free slot
  • in some locations special events might be available if the player has a creature with him
