The player and maybe other NPC should be able to change their body structure, f.e. a human morphs to an anthro-wolf. # MorphState - For each possible body structure, the character keeps track of a morpstate ranging from 0 to 100%. F.e a morphstate for human, anthrowolf, feral wolf,... - Each time the character is exposed to a morphing source, f.e. drinking a werwolf-potion, that morphstate is increased. - If the morpstate reaches 100%, the character is morphed to this creature. After the morph all morphstates a set back to 0. - their might be notifications to the player at certain levels, f.e. 25,50, 75% telling him that he should be aware of the changes.
