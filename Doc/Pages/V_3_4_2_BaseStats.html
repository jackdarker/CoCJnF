BaseAttributes

Each Player/NPC/Monster has the following Stats

  • Agility
  • Perception
  • Stamina
  • Strength
  • Intelligence
  • Willpower ??
  • Charisma
  • Corruption  ???
  • Luck ??

The attributes depends on initial character generation, species-traits and effects caused by equipment, potions, perks.

Actions should rely one a combination of Attributes, f.e. climbing might depend on Agility & Stamina scavenging depends on Perception & Agility....

DerivedStats

Depending on Basevalues, the follwoing Stats for combat are calculated

  • Hitpoints (HP)
    • defines your overall health
    • = Stamina+Strength
  • Actionpoints (AP)
    • how many actions you can do in one round
    • =Min(Agility , Strength)
  • Damage output (for different damage types??)
    • your bare handed damage
    • =Strength+Intelligence
  • Damage resistance (for different damage types??)
    • your natural armor
    • =Stamina+Willpower
  • Fatigue (FP)
    • defines your energy reserves
    • =Avg(Stamina,Agility,Willpower)
