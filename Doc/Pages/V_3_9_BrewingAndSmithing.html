Brewing, Smithing

This feature is added later.

  • The player can create stuff from existing items
  • For this he needs an IDEA like "I could make a leather armor"
  • This idea might be refealed by talking to NPC,finding ingridients, reading books or other interactions.
  • He might then start experimenting on the proper tool, f.e. a brewer
  • He can select from multiple choice of what items to combine in what ratio.
  • Experimenting has a chance to fail and consume the spend items. The chance depends on how close the ratio of the items matches the correct recipe, how many items he spends (up to a limit) his skill,a.s.o
  • A succesful experiment doesnt mean the player knows how to safely produce the result again.
  • But he might recover an RECIPE that increases successchance greatly, uncovers the correct combination or reduces the amount of materials to spend

Assign the following Tag keywords to items so taht they can be found

  • Alchemy:Recipe
  • Alchemy:Ingridient
  • Alchemy:Result
  • Alchemy:Idea
