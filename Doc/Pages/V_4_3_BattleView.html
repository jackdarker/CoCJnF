This view is used when exploring/scavenging.

Elements on this view are:

  • one-screen-size gameview
  • the number of waves left
  • player-stats
  • Action-menu
  • enemy-stats
  • After a wave is done, blackscreen to next wave or final result-page.

Game-View

  • player on left side,enemys right
  • at players turn, on Fire1 the action menu opens up

Action-Menu

  • a list of buttons for selecting actions
  • text-box to show info about the action
  • possible actions depend on: equipment, effects, perks,...
  • has a back button to close the list
  • actions that are in cooldown are disabled and their cooldown time is shown
  • actions that require to much action-points or are disabled by effects, are disabled
  • the action is executed immedialty

Possible actions:

Repeat last move

  • shortcut for repeating the last action

Attack

  • shows a list of all possible actions valid for attack

Defend

  • shows a list of all possible actions valid for defend

Sense

  • executes a Sense-Move that shows enemy-stats

Inventory

  • shows the inventory where items can be used

Info

  • shows detailed stats including effects for the selected player/enemy

End turn

  • do nothing to recover fatigue and switch turn

Flee

  • try emergency escape to camp of player choice
  • on success player will loose collected money and some collected items

Player Stats

  • bargraph for armor,HP, Fatigue , AP
  • icons for effects

Enemy Stats

  • bargraph for armor,HP, Fatigue , AP
  • icons for effects

Detailed Enemy Stats

  • more details are revealed with the Sense-Move
  • species, attack&defend-moves, effect-details, weakness/strong

Waves

  • how many waves are left
